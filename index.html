Rules

Blackjack is a game that involves 2 players. Both get 2 cards to start the game.

Goal is to get 21 points without going over or that player/dealer loses.

Face cards ie J, Q, K are worth 10 points and Ace is worth 1 or 11 points.

Player can choose to "HIT" to take another card to add to their total or "STAY" to keep their current total count.
